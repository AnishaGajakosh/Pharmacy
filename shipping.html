Shipping Policy

We aim to deliver medicines and healthcare products quickly and safely to your doorstep.

Delivery Timelines

  • Orders are processed within 24 hours.
  • Delivery within metro cities: 2–4 business days.
  • Delivery to other areas: 4–7 business days.

Shipping Charges

We offer free shipping on orders above ₹499. For orders below this amount, a nominal delivery fee applies.

Order Tracking

You will receive tracking details via email/SMS once your order has been dispatched.

Contact

If you have questions about shipping, please contact us at support@a1medical.com.
