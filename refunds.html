Cancellation & Refund Policy

Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled only before dispatch.
  • Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.

Returns

  • We do not accept returns for medicines unless the item is damaged, defective, or incorrect.
  • Products must be unopened, unused, and in original packaging.

Refunds

  • Refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days after verification.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.

Contact

For refund/cancellation requests, please contact us at support@carekart.com.
