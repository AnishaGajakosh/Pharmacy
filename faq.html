Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +
Simply browse our products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout.
Do you deliver all over India? +
Yes, we deliver across India with reliable shipping partners.
Are prescriptions required? +
Yes, for certain prescription medicines, you must upload a valid prescription at checkout.
How can I track my order? +
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS/email to monitor your delivery status.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, wallets, and cash on delivery (COD) in select locations.
How long does delivery take? +
Delivery usually takes 2–5 business days depending on your location. Metro cities may get next-day delivery.
Can I cancel or modify my order? +
Yes, orders can be canceled or modified before they are shipped. Once shipped, cancellations are not possible.
What if I receive a damaged or wrong product? +
If you receive a wrong or damaged item, contact our support team within 48 hours for a replacement or refund.
Is my personal information safe? +
Yes, we use SSL encryption and strict privacy policies to protect your data. We never sell your information.
How do I contact customer support? +
You can reach us via the Contact Us page, email, or our 24/7 customer care helpline.
