Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. At CareKart, we are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring it is used only for legitimate purposes such as order processing, delivery, and customer support.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, email, phone number, shipping/billing address.
  • Account information: login credentials (securely encrypted).
  • Payment information: handled securely via trusted gateways (we never store card details).
  • Order history and preferences to improve your experience.
  • Technical data like IP address, browser type, and cookies for site functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders safely and on time.
  • Provide customer support and respond to queries.
  • Send important notifications (order confirmations, delivery updates).
  • Improve our website, services, and product offerings.
  • Comply with pharmacy regulations and legal requirements.

Data Security

We use secure servers, SSL encryption, and strict access controls to protect your data. Only authorized staff can access customer information, and they are trained in data protection practices.

Sharing of Information

We never sell or rent your data to third parties. Information is shared only with:

  • Delivery partners for shipping your orders.
  • Payment processors for secure transactions.
  • Regulatory authorities if required by law.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve your shopping experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. You may also opt-out of promotional emails or request a copy of the data we hold about you by contacting us.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
